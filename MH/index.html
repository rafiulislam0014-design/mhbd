About Master Home

Welcome to Master Home, where comfort meets convenience. We are dedicated to providing you with the best accommodation experience in Dhaka.

Our mission is to create a home away from home for all our guests. With years of experience in hospitality, we understand what students and working professionals need for a comfortable stay.

Our team of professional staff is available 24/7 to ensure your stay is perfect. From check-in to check-out, we're here to make your experience memorable.

Located in Sukrabad, Dhaka, Master Home offers easy access to major IELTS centers, mentors, Japanese language centers, admission students, universities, business districts, and transportation hubs. We provide a safe, secure, and comfortable environment for all our residents.
